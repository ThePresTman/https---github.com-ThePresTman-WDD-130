Purpose

Our purpose is to help bring a easy to use and friendly workout website that will change how workout website and apps will be looked at. Because of our goal, not only will we provide workouts selected and aimed to achieve that growth, we will also provide a real time graph that helps visualize your strength growing over time.

The audience is anyone who works out, wants to start working out, and any type of athlete.
